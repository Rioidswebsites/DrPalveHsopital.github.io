AMBP (Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring)

Ambulatory blood pressure monitoring is a test to obtain blood pressure at comfort level of patient in their home which masks patient’s anxiety at hospital or clinic and helps clinicians to diagnose hypertension and gives a clear view of cardiovascular status of the patient.

  • Blood Pressure Variability Assessment
  • Evaluation of Treatment Effectiveness
  • Patient Compliance and Awareness
  • Long-Term Monitoring
order test

Continuous Glucose Monitoring System

CGMS is a device with a sensor which measures Diabetic patient’s interstitial glucose level every few minutes, which is the glucose found in the fluid between the cells. A transmitter wirelessly sends the information to a monitor.

  • Real-Time CGMS
  • Integrated CGMS
  • Flash CGMS
  • Wearable CGMS
order test

PFT / SPIROMETRY STRESS TEST (TMT)

Pulmonary function test is a non-invasive test to measure lung volume, capacity, gas exchange, rates of flow and this information can help your healthcare provider to diagnose and decide the treatment of certain lung disorders.

  • Standard Exercise Stress Test
  • Pharmacological Stress Test
  • Stress Echocardiogram
  • Nuclear Stress Test
